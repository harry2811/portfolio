About Me

With over 3 years of experience in Shopify development, I specialize in creating high-performing, customized e-commerce solutions tailored to each brand’s unique goals. My work spans advanced Shopify theme customization, custom-coded features, and building fully responsive landing pages that convert. I have a deep command of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Liquid, and AJAX, which allows me to bring even the most complex design and functionality ideas to life with precision. I’ve also worked on Shopify Plus projects using Hydrogen, have some hands-on experience with React, and am familiar with popular Shopify page builders like PageFly, Shogun, and others. Whether it’s modifying existing themes or developing bespoke features from scratch, I always aim to deliver clean, scalable code that enhances both aesthetics and usability.

Beyond the front end, I also work extensively with Shopify Admin APIs to automate workflows and integrate third-party services. I use Git for version control to manage code changes efficiently, enabling smooth collaboration and safe deployments. My focus on Shopify speed optimization ensures that the storefronts I build load fast and perform well across devices—an essential factor for both SEO and user experience. With a strong problem-solving mindset and an eye for detail, I’m committed to delivering Shopify solutions that not only meet technical requirements but also drive measurable business results.
